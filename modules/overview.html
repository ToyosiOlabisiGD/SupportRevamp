Customer Support Training
Training Overview

This training program will take you through a structured 6–8 week path to prepare you for live case handling in your assigned support role.

Your path includes:

  • Onboarding – setup and orientation
  • Core Training – tools, tags, case flow
  • Moderation – Jobseeker or Employer content rules
  • Support – Jobseeker or Employer workflows
  • Profile Changes – for Employer path only

What success looks like:

  • Completing weekly progress checklists
  • Shadowing and being shadowed
  • Passing quizzes and trainer reviews
